Furniture Assembly Pricing Guide – FurnitureFix

Looking for affordable and professional furniture assembly in the UK? At FurnitureFix, we provide reliable, high-quality assembly services for a wide range of furniture items. Our goal is to make your life easier by delivering fast, efficient, and hassle-free service — all at competitive prices.

How Our Pricing Works

We understand that every piece of furniture is unique, so our pricing is designed to be transparent, flexible, and fair:

  • Per Item Pricing: We don’t charge by the hour. Instead, we agree on a fixed price for each item (or set of items) before we start, so there are no surprises.
  • Customised Approach: Every customer and piece of furniture is different. We assess the design, size, and complexity of your furniture and provide the best possible price tailored to your needs.
  • Clean-Up Included: After assembly, we make sure your space is tidy. No mess, no stress.
  • Discounts for Multiple Items: Got more than one item to assemble? We offer slight discounts for multiple items.
